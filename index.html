About Me

Two Worlds,
One Mind

I am an Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning engineering student at Bhausaheb Vartak Polytechnic, fascinated by how machines can learn, reason, and create. I work with Computer Vision, Deep Learning, and Data Science to build systems that make sense of the world.
